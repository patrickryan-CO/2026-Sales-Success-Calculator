Strategic Intent

This section is the definitive foundation of your success plan. It is the tactical roadmap that you will execute throughout the fiscal year. This is not a checklist; it is your professional commitment on paper.

You have been provided with a 45-minute deep-work window to complete this section. Specificity dictates success. Use this time to move beyond general ideas and commit to the tactical behaviors that will drive your specific efficiency targets.

The SMART Framework:
S: Specific; Define exactly what you will do. Avoid vague terms like "improve."
M: Measurable; Add a number or specific milestone so success isn't subjective.
A: Achievable; Reality check: ensure the goal is realistic.
R: Relevant; Align with your 2026 targets.
T: Time-bound; Set a clear deadline or frequency.
How to Apply:

1. Sharpen Your Focus: State exactly what part of the sales process you will own.

2. Define the Evidence: Attach a clear number or milestone so the result is binary.

3. Lock in the Clock: Assign an expiration date to prevent indefinite wish-lists.
