Strategic Intent

This Action Plan is your foundation. These commitments represent the personal roadmap management will use to align resources and provide targeted coaching.

The SMART Goal Framework (How to write them):
S: Specific - Define exactly what you will do. Avoid vague terms.
M: Measurable - Add a number or specific milestone.
A: Achievable - Reality check. Ensure the goal is realistic.
R: Relevant - Align with your role and objectives.
T: Time-bound - Set a clear deadline for execution.
